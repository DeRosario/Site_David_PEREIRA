History

Wild Circus was founded in 2008 in Portugal by David Pereira. At beginning the circus was named "Santa Maria Circus" like the historical vessel which allowed the maritime explorer Christopher Columbus to go in America. Set in France for one year, its tour across France will start in Paris in March and will end up in January in Marseille.
